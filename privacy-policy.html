Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

MoltCast ("we", "our", or "the app") is an automated video publishing tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application to publish content to TikTok.

Information We Collect

MoltCast collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • TikTok Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens required to publish videos to your TikTok account. These are stored locally and never transmitted to any third-party servers.
  • Content Metadata: Information about videos you create and publish, including titles, descriptions, and publish timestamps.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok's API to publish videos on your behalf
  • Track which content has been published to avoid duplicates

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. MoltCast does not operate any servers and does not transmit your personal information to any external services other than TikTok's official API for video publishing.

Third-Party Services

MoltCast integrates with TikTok's API. When you authenticate and publish content, you are subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Authentication tokens and content metadata are retained locally until you delete them. You can remove all stored data by deleting the application's data directory.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can revoke TikTok access at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • You can delete all local data by removing the application's data files

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
